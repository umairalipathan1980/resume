Profile

I am a researcher and AI consultant with over 15 years of experience spanning academia and industry across Austria, Germany, Finland, and Pakistan. With a Ph.D. in Information Technology from Austria and leadership experience in Pakistan, I currently serve as a Senior Researcher and AI Advisor at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences in Helsinki, Finland. My expertise encompasses applied artificial intelligence, machine learning, and generative AI, with a proven track record of securing international funding, managing R&D projects, and publishing high-impact research.

As an AI consultant and developer for the Finnish AI Region (FAIR) project, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical business applications. I specialize in designing AI roadmaps, developing proof-of-concepts, and providing technical consultancy to startups and SMEs across various sectors. I also specialize in establishing research networks and collaborating effectively with diverse research teams, industry stakeholders, and government agencies across Europe and beyond.

My current research focus on AI agents, RAG, MCP, and knowledge extraction & processing using LLms. I am passionate about democratizing AI knowledge and ensuring responsible AI adoption in business and education sectors.
